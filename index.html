Feb 21, 2025 		ABOUT MANIPUR UNIVERSITY

Manipur University was established on 5th June 1980 under the Manipur University Act, 1980 (Manipur Act 8 of 1980), as a teaching-cum-affiliating University at Imphal with territorial jurisdiction over the whole state of Manipur. It was converted into a central university w.e.f. 13/10/2005. The Manipur University Act No. 54 of 2005 received the assent of the President on 28/12/2005.
